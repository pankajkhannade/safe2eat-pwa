Safe2Eat

Version 1.1 — MVP

Open source food safety checker. Scan a barcode → get ingredient analysis against FSSAI, EU, and FDA standards. Built for Indian families.

Data Sources

🌍Open Food Facts — 3M+ products, CC BY-SA 4.0
🇪🇺EU Food Safety Authority (EFSA) — additive classifications
🇺🇸US FDA — GRAS list & food additive regulations
🇮🇳FSSAI India — EU proxy used for MVP (FSSAI data being compiled)
🥬Vegetarian Society UK — animal-derived ingredient guide

Key Features

📷Barcode scanning (Android Chrome), manual entry, search by name
🌙Light / Dark theme with persistent preference
📴Offline mode — cached results when no connection
🌐Hindi + English interface

⚠️ Safety ratings are algorithmic estimates based on publicly available regulatory data. Always verify with official sources. This app advises, it does not diagnose. For medical advice, consult a healthcare professional.
